Festive Scrambled Eggs

Description

Every bit as quick as scrambled eggs are meant to be, this hearty dish— with red pimientos and green parsley or chives— is nice for hectic Christmas mornings.

Ingredients

  • 12 large eggs
  • 1-1/2 cup 2% milk, divided
  • 1/2 to 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 tablespoons diced pimientos
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley or chives
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup butter, cubed

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, beat eggs and 1 cup milk
  2. dd the salt, pepper, pimientos and parsley
  3. In a small bowl, combine flour and remaining 1/2 cup milk until smooth; stir into egg mixture
  4. In a large cast-iron or other heavy skillet, melt butter over medium heat
  5. Add egg mixture
  6. Cook and stir over medium heat until the eggs are completely set
