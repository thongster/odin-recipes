Cheesy Potato Egg Bake

Description

I whipped up this cozy egg bake with potato crowns for an easy “brinner.” Use add-ins you like – sweet peppers, onions, broccoli, carrots. The possibilities are endless. —Amy Lents, Grand Forks, North Dakota

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bulk lean turkey breakfast sausage
  • 1-3/4 cups sliced baby portobello mushrooms, chopped
  • 4 cups fresh spinach, coarsely chopped
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup 2% milk
  • Dash seasoned salt
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 6 cups frozen potato crowns

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°
  2. In a large skillet, cook sausage over medium heat until no longer pink, 5-7 minutes, breaking into crumbles
  3. Add mushrooms and spinach; cook until mushrooms are tender and spinach is wilted, 2-4 minutes longer
  4. Spoon sausage mixture into a greased 13x9-in. baking dish
  5. In a large bowl, whisk eggs, milk and seasoned salt until blended; pour over sausage mixture
  6. Layer with cheese and potato crowns
  7. Bake, uncovered, until set and top is crisp, 45-50 minutes
